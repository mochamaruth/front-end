the architect

Surprisingly The house was designed by one architect,Jorn Utzon. His design was chosen from two hundred and thirty three entries in the 1956 international competition.

the house

The house has won recognition from all over the world . These include the Royal Australian Institute Gold Award to Jorn Utzon in 1973 upon its completion, the United Kingdom Institution of Structural Engineers Special Award in 1973, and a Commemorative international Pritzker for his contributions to architecture and in recognition of his masterpiece, the Sydney Opera House.
