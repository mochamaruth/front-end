akirachix

Akirachix is an organization whose main aim is to empower women. Akirachix was started in 2010 with it's main aim being to support the women in tech and also increase the number of women in tech. Since 2010, the program has been able to graduate many students who undertook various courses including backend and frontend development, graphic design, mobile applications development and entrepreneurship.

enrollment

To enroll for the program students who were interested filled in application forms which were distributed to various institutions including schools. A selection was made and the shortlisted students did an English and Mathematics test. After this the list was narrowed down to almost a hundred students who did an interview and thirty one students were cosen to form the class of 2015.
